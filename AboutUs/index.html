About Us

At Will’s Precision Layer Prints, we believe in bringing your ideas to life—layer by layer. Founded by Will, our mission is to deliver high-quality, custom 3D printed creations at affordable prices. Every order is handled with care, precision, and a personal touch.

Whether you need a unique gift, a replacement part, or a custom design, we’re here to make it happen.

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect: We collect customer information including name, shipping address, email address, and order details when you place an order.

How We Use Your Information: Your information is used solely for processing, producing, and shipping your order, and for warranty purposes.

Data Retention: We store order details—including your name, shipping address, email, and order information—for at least one (1) year after the order date. This allows us to process returns, exchanges, and warranty claims. After this period, your data may be permanently deleted from both customer and administrator systems.

Sharing Your Information: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except when required by law or for shipping purposes.

Security: We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
